# Sephiroth, Fabled SOLDIER - Sideboard
# Budget alternatives for testing and meta adjustments (~$3-5 total)

## Alternative Sacrifice Outlets
- Spawning Pit ($0.20) - Artifact sac outlet, makes tokens
- Mind Slash ($0.15) - Sac outlet with discard effect
- Phyrexian Plaguelord ($0.15) - Sac outlet that shrinks creatures

## Alternative Death Payoffs
- Poison-Tip Archer ($0.15) - Reach + death drain
- Agent of the Iron Throne ($0.15) - Background with death drain
- Grim Guardian ($0.10) - Constellation drain (works with Bastion)

## Alternative Recursion
- Unearth ($0.75) - Cheap reanimation, cycles
- Ghoulcaller's Chant ($0.10) - Return 2 zombies
- Tortured Existence ($0.50) - Repeatable swap

## Alternative Token Generators  
- Ogre Slumlord ($0.35) - Token generator on creature death
- Ghoulish Procession ($0.15) - Tokens when nontoken creatures die
- Skeletal Vampire ($0.25) - Token generator + sac outlet

## Meta Adjustments
- Nihil Spellbomb ($0.20) - Targeted graveyard hate
- Relic of Progenitus ($0.40) - More graveyard hate
- Duress ($0.10) - Hand disruption
- Pharika's Libation ($0.10) - Enchantment removal

## Alternative Win Conditions
- Vampire Nighthawk ($0.40) - Efficient creature, good devotion
- Corrupt ($0.15) - Direct drain spell
- Consume Spirit ($0.10) - X drain spell

## Protection Options
- Imp's Mischief ($1.50) - Redirect targeting spell
- Undying Malice ($0.10) - Instant creature protection
- Supernatural Stamina ($0.10) - Return on death

Total estimated sideboard cost: ~$4.50
