VULNERABILITY 1: Graveyard Hate
================================
Threat Level: HIGH
Common Hate Cards:
- Rest in Peace (exile GY, prevent GY)
- Bojuka Bog (exile target GY)
- Tormod's Crypt (0 cost artifact, exile GY)
- Soul-Guide Lantern (1 cost artifact, exile GY)
- Relic of Progenitus (1 cost artifact, exile GY)

Mitigation Strategies:
1. Instant-Speed Encore Response
   - Hold up 1UB + exile fuel
   - In response to Bojuka Bog trigger, encore target
   - In response to Tormod activation, encore target
   
2. Graveyard Insurance
   - Perpetual Timepiece: shuffle GY back into library if needed
   - We have this card already ✓
   
3. Backup Win Conditions
   - Altar of Dementia: mill without graveyard reliance
   - Kokusho/Dire Fleet Ravager: can hardcast if needed
   - Sidisi: tutor for answers
   
4. Graveyard Hate for Opponents
   - Bojuka Bog in our lands ✓
   - Use it politically or to stop reanimator opponents

Assessment: MITIGATED
- Have instant-speed encore capability (13 instant-speed mills)
- Have Perpetual Timepiece as insurance
- Have alternative win paths


VULNERABILITY 2: Commander Removal
===================================
Threat Level: MEDIUM
Common Removal:
- Swords to Plowshares, Path to Exile
- Murder, Go for the Throat, etc.
- Board wipes

Commander Tax Problem:
- Araumi: 1UB (3 mana)
- Recast 1: 5 mana
- Recast 2: 7 mana
- Gets expensive quickly

Mitigation Strategies:
1. Protection Equipment
   - Lightning Greaves: shroud, haste ✓
   - Swiftfoot Boots: hexproof, haste ✓
   - Both give immediate encore capability
   
2. Alternative Game Plan
   - Can win without Araumi via:
     * Altar of Dementia loops
     * Hardcast Kokusho/Gray Merchant with devotion
     * Sidisi tutor chains
   
3. Timing Protection
   - Play Araumi with protection ready
   - Encore same turn with haste boots
   
4. Death Insurance
   - Kaya's Ghostform: returns Araumi when destroyed ✓
   - Supernatural Stamina: regenerate effect ✓

Assessment: WELL MITIGATED
- 4 protection cards for Araumi
- Multiple win paths without commander


VULNERABILITY 3: Board Wipes
=============================
Threat Level: LOW-MEDIUM
Common Wipes:
- Wrath of God, Damnation
- Crux of Fate, Blasphemous Act
- Cyclonic Rift

Impact on Strategy:
- Encore tokens exile anyway (don't care about wipes)
- Only care about protecting Araumi
- Can encore in response to wipe (get value first)

Mitigation Strategies:
1. Encore Timing
   - Encore on end step before our turn
   - Even if wiped, we got triple ETB value
   - Example: Encore Mulldrifter end step, draw 6, then wipe happens
   
2. Graveyard Reload
   - Wipes help us by filling opponent GYs
   - Our creatures dying fills our GY for more encores
   
3. Recursion
   - Archaeomancer/Mnemonic Wall get back spells
   - Can encore recursion creatures to replay answers
   
4. Protection for Araumi
   - Lightning Greaves/Swiftfoot Boots keep Araumi alive ✓

Assessment: LOW THREAT
- Encore timing makes wipes beneficial for us
- Protection keeps Araumi alive


VULNERABILITY 4: Fast Combo Decks
==================================
Threat Level: MEDIUM
Common Combos:
- Turn 3-4 infinite combos
- Storm decks
- Turbo reanimator

Our Speed:
- Turn 3: Araumi
- Turn 4-5: First encore (need mana + mill setup)
- Turn 5-6: Kill with Gray Merchant encore

Mitigation Strategies:
1. Interaction Suite
   - 5 removal pieces + counterspells from creatures ✓
   - Siren Stormtamer: counter spell targeting permanent ✓
   - Can encore removal creatures (3x Fleshbag = 3 sacrifices)
   
2. Disruptive ETBs
   - Fleshbag Marauder: force sacrifice
   - Plaguecrafter: force sacrifice/discard
   - Man-o'-War: bounce key piece
   
3. Speed Up Our Clock
   - Turn 4 encore possible with:
     * Turn 1: Land, Sol Ring, Dimir Signet
     * Turn 2: Land, Araumi
     * Turn 3: Land, instant mill (3-5 cards), encore target
   - Turn 5 win with Gray Merchant encore

Assessment: MODERATE THREAT
- We're fast enough (turn 5-6 wins)
- Have interaction to slow opponents
- Could use 1-2 more counterspells for budget


VULNERABILITY 5: Mill/Exile Effects
====================================
Threat Level: LOW
Threats:
- Opponents milling us (helps us!)
- Opponents exiling our graveyard (covered in Vulnerability 1)
- Opponents exiling our library (rare)

Assessment: BENEFICIAL OR MITIGATED
- Mill helps our strategy
- Exile covered by Vulnerability 1 mitigation
